Trans Pride Day 2024

Trans Pride Day 2024, "Trans Pride in the Park," is a pop-up event scheduled for November 23, 2024, from 12 noon to 4 pm, and will take place in Stoner Park.

Get to know the local trans community, familiarize yourself with local trans-affirming organizations, and just chill out and celebrate the joy of the transgender experience!

There is no cost to attend. Swag and community information will be available from the following organizations:

  • Alabama Transgender Rights Action Coalition
  • Trans North Alabama
  • Weird Kids
  • At The Root Collective
  • Thrive Alabama
  • PFLAG Huntsville
  • League of Women Voters of Tennessee Valley
  • Common Grounds at AAMU
  • North Alabama Democratic Socialists of America
  • Free Mom Hugs - Huntsville
  • Lighthouse Clinical Social Work

In addition, we'll have a station set up to help people take their passport photos free of charge. If you need to update your passport, we'll help you with this step of the process.

This is a laid-back, community-driven event. Please feel free to bring food to picnic with and share with others if you'd like!
