It’s a new year and a new chance to unite for the greater good!

Join us at Shenanigans Comedy Theatre to celebrate women, with an emphasis on our trans sisters and women of color. Become familiar with resources available in the area, collaborate with supportive local organizers, learn how we can be productive towards our intersectional legislative goals, and last but not least, chill out with your community. Bring the family - we’ll have refreshments on-site and activities for the kids!

  • When: Saturday 24 February 2024, 2:30pm - 4:30pm
  • Where: Shenanigans Comedy Theatre

This event is open to the public. No registration or RSVP is required to attend.

We invite local organizations to participate by tabling and by giving your elevator pitch on stage, if you’d like! There will be an opportunity to introduce your organization to the attendees, connect with other like-minded organizations, and spread the word about your org to the people of Huntsville.
