ALTRAC is just one of many organizations in Alabama working to advance progressive values and stop bad legislation from passing. If you are interested in keeping up to date on other legislation and events throughout the state, please check out these organizations:

  • The Alabama ACLU is our local chapter of the American Civil Liberties Union. They work on issues around free speech, discrimination based on protected classes, and police misconduct.
  • United Women of Color, based in Madison County, is working to create an inclusive community by building relationships and facilitating authentic connections to address the community's changing needs.
  • The League of Women Voters of Alabama is working to protect and advance voting rights in our state.
  • Alabama Arise is a statewide, member-led organization advancing public policies to improve the lives of Alabamians who are marginalized by poverty.
  • Alabama Values Progress is a media advocacy organization providing frequent updates on what's happening in the legislative session.
