Come join us after the March Without Labels for an in-person Advocacy Day! We’ll assemble together at the capital, gas each other up, then go talk to our own representatives in-person, in their offices.

Tell them your personal story about how you or someone you love will be affected by these bills. Most of these lawmakers have never knowingly met a trans person, or someone who has trans kids, or someone who’s impacted by Don’t Say Gay.

This is our time to show them that queer people and our allies exist in the districts they serve, and we’re paying attention.
