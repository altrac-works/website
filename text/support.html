Here are some ways you can help the Alabama transgender community today. Thank you for your support!

  1. The Center at Shenanigans

    The Center provides and finds resources for the diverse North Alabama community. Donate clothing to the LGBTQ+ Closet, or contact the Diversity Center if you'd like to provide resources.

    Visit the Diversity Center website Donate to the Huntsville LGBTQ+ Closet

  2. Rocket City Medic Collective

    This group works to provide trans folks in North Alabama with medical supplies necessary for HRT.

    Donate money Donate medical supplies

  3. Weird Kids

    This group is here for the teens and kids that live life on the weird side. Show your support by attending their events in Huntsville and the surrounding areas.

    Learn about upcoming Weird Kids events

  4. TAKE Birmingham

    TAKE is a direct services organization focusing on breaking down barriers and setting standards within the Birmingham community for trans-related issues, like workplace discrimination, housing advocacy, and more.

    Donate to TAKE

  5. The Knights & Orchids Society

    TKO is a direct services organization to help more Black trans, same-gender-loving, and Black queer folks access high-quality primary care and endocrinology services.

    Donate to TKO
